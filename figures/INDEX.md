| [dpo_numbers_results.md](dpo_numbers_results.md) | **Thread B #36 — the SFT↔DPO bridge.** DPO on the cat number-sequences (chosen = cat-teacher numbers, rejected = base numbers for the same prompt) is a near-null *only at the x26 scale* (~2%); **at 250k it transfers up to 66%** (rank 4; vs SFT's 84–89% / owl-DPO's 38–81%) — not lr-starvation (margins 8.9–17.1, ‖ΔW‖ 2.4–47.8, margin doesn't predict elicitation), but the *mechanism is open*: the contrast *isolates* the cat tilt (∝ P_cat−P_base) rather than cancelling it (the #25 algebra), the null was the small-N/SNR regime (#11), **resolved by the 250k scale-up**: low-rank DPO transfers ~66% with a clean rank·LR≈8e-4 law, coherently (400-story Sonnet audit). Empirically completes the 2×2; includes the generation / system-prompt design clarifications (cat prompt at gen only, Qwen-default at train/eval, verified via `maybe_apply_chat_template`) and the full 24-cell table. |
